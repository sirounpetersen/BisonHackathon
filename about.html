Who We Are

We are a non profit organization dedicated to builing a more personalized relationhip with you and your low income community. In our current environment with the wealth gap at a steady increase with no sign of de-acceleration, and the current pandemic that has impaced minorities of low income the most, it is imperative that charity becomes more than sending a check to an organization. Now more than ever, there is a demand for us as a society to be more empathetic towards our neighbor. If our local communities struggle, the solution to getting past this pandemic will be farther away. Donationship is dedicated to creating healthier relationships and sustainability with peoples of low income, and their community.
